Dr. Stephen Akintayo
Chairman, Gtext Holdings | Founder, Stephen Akintayo Foundation | Forbes Best of Africa Leading Investment Coach

Dr. Stephen Akintayo is Africa's most influential investment coach and a globally recognized entrepreneur, author, and philanthropist. He is the Chairman of Gtext Holdings, a multinational conglomerate with subsidiaries in real estate, agriculture, technology, and digital marketing. Under his leadership, Gtext Holdings has expanded its presence across Nigeria, the United States, the United Kingdom, and the United Arab Emirates.

Born on January 28, 1986, in the Gonge area of Maiduguri, Borno State, Nigeria, Dr. Akintayo's early life was marked by poverty. Despite these challenges, he pursued education diligently, earning a BSc in Microbiology from Olabisi Onabanjo University. His entrepreneurial journey began during his university days, selling eBooks and GNLD supplements. In 2008, he founded Gilead Balm Group, which evolved into Gtext Holdings.

Dr. Akintayo is also the founder of the Stephen Akintayo Foundation, which aims to empower entrepreneurs through mentorship and training programs. The foundation's vision is to create 1 million millionaires by 2050, with 70% from African heritage. In 2020, he launched the Stephen Akintayo Online Wealth University, offering courses on real estate investment, business structure, and more.

An accomplished author, Dr. Akintayo has written over 45 books, including Billionaire Habits, Millionaire Bloggers, and Billionaire Codes.
